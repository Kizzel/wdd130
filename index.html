Ghana

Ghana is a country located in West Africa, known for its rich culture, diverse wildlife, and vibrant music scene.

"Hi my name is Ezekiel K. D Kufoalor, a native of Ghana, I reside in Tema, a small town near Accra. I come from a tribe called Ewe in the Volta Region. I have a strong passion for art, particularly music, drawing, painting, and video games. I also enjoys exploring different cuisines and I enjoy readeing. I'm the youngest of six siblings, including two half-siblings. I served a mission in Johannesburg, South Africa, from 2014 to 2016. While new to programming, I find it challenging but rewarding. My goal is to complete the course and pursue a career as a front-end developer in the United States."
