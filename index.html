Welcome to River Rush Rafting Co.! Our mission is to provide unforgettable outdoor adventures while promoting environmental stewardship and safety.

History

Founded in 2005 by outdoor enthusiasts, River Rush Rafting Co. started as a small operation with a single raft. Over the years, we've grown into one of the leading rafting companies in the region, serving thousands of thrill-seekers each year.
